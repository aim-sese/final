a little about me

i am a creative born and raised in the east bay area. my artistic specialties lie in graphic design and creative writing, but i am always, always looking to learn more and expand my capabilities. i try to engage with as many different kinds of media as i can, but i have a particular love towards musical theatre, standup comedy, and japanese literature.

i attended cal state east bay from 2020 - 2023, where i had the opportunity to try my hand at a variety of endeavours ranging from student journalism to social media management. while i don't currently intend to pursue any of those fields in a professional capacity, i will always be grateful for the opportunities i had there, and find it a delight to continue to use them in my capacities as an indie creative.

these days, you can usually find me stretching out my plantar fascia, mixing fruits and granola into greek yogurt, or scouring ebay listings for vintage clothing. but if you need a more concrete way to reach me, then check out this page.
